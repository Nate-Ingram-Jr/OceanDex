# OceanDex
A real-world Pokédex for the ocean built to educate, protect, and empower every kind of sea enthusiast.
# What is OceanDex?
OceanDex is a comprehensive sea life reference app inspired by the idea of a Pokédex: but for real, living ocean creatures. From the Atlantic Blue Crab found in the Chesapeake Bay to the endangered Whale Shark crossing open oceans, OceanDex puts detailed, accurate information about any sea creature at your fingertips. The ocean is vast and largely unknown to most people, and OceanDex exists to change that.
# Who Is It For?
OceanDex is designed for anyone who interacts with or is curious about the sea:
Fishermen: know exactly what you’ve caught, whether it’s legal to keep, and what size and bag limits apply in your state
Marine biologists: a quick-reference field companion for species identification, conservation status, and habitat data
Hobbyists & ocean enthusiasts: explore and learn about creatures you’ve seen, heard about, or are simply curious about
# What OceanDex Does
The app covers fish, sharks, shellfish, rays, and anything else found beneath the surface. For every species, users can access:
Detailed species profiles: scientific name, size, depth range, diet, habitat, and lifespan
Conservation status: IUCN(International Union for Conservation of Nature) Red List classification, population trend, and an awareness fact to highlight lesser-known threats
Legal regulations by state: harvest laws, minimum sizes, bag limits, permit requirements, and the authority that enforces them
Habitat & range maps: where the species lives, what water conditions it prefers, and seasonal migration patterns
ID Scanner: point your camera at a sea creature to identify it instantly, even from a photo
# Why It Matters
Most people are unaware that over 31% of shark species worldwide are threatened with extinction and sharks are just one example. Horseshoe crabs, Atlantic sturgeon, and countless other species face serious pressure from overfishing, habitat loss, and climate change, yet most people fishing or swimming near them have no idea. OceanDex brings these realities to the surface. By making conservation data and legal information easy to find and understand, the app helps people make informed decisions whether that means releasing a catch, reporting a sighting, or simply understanding the creature in front of them.
